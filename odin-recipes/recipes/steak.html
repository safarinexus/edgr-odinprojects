Nothing like a nice hunk of meat.


Description:

Just some nice cow meat, add heat till it browns and is delicious

Ingredients:

  • The steak
  • Salt n Peppa
  • Butter or Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Heat up your frying pan until the oil/butter starts to smoke a lil
  2. Put that hunk of meat on it
  3. Flip occasionally
  4. Feel for how well you want it done!
