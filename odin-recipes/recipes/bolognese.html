Bolognese! (spaghetti, fettucini, whatever you want it with!)


Description:

It's tomatey, it's meaty, it's pastay, what's not to like!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Diced Meat
  • Tomato Paste

Steps:

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Put your diced meat onto a frying pan that's hot
  3. Pour in tomato paste
  4. Mix it all together well!
